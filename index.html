About Me

I’m Melissa Diaz, a 26‑year‑old cybersecurity student with a proud Cuban background. Since I was 16, I’ve balanced work, school, and family responsibilities with one purpose: to build a better future and honor the people who sacrificed so much for me. I’ve always wanted to be recognized for who I truly am—without barriers—and technology has been my path to do that.

Cybersecurity opened my eyes to a world I can help create: one where people, data, and organizations are safer. I love translating frameworks into action—turning policies and controls into daily habits that actually protect others. I’m especially drawn to SOC workflows, governance/risk/compliance (GRC), and clear communication that brings teams together.

I’m based in Miami and seeking entry‑level roles such as Security Analyst, SOC Analyst, or GRC Analyst. My goal is simple: keep learning, contribute with integrity, and use cybersecurity to open doors—for my family, my community, and the teams I serve.
